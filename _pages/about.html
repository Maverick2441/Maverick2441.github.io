Sarah Johnson is a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where she studies combustion and chemical kinetics. She works in the High Temperature Gasdynamics Laboratory under Professor Ron Hanson.

Sarah received her B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Alabama in 2013 (Roll Tide!), with minors in history and the Computer-Based Honors Program (now Randall Research Scholars). She received a Mitchell Scholarship to study for her M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Queen's University in Belfast, Northern Ireland, where she graduated with distinction.
